Our Mission

At THE HUB, our mission is to make people happy by providing access to iconic movies and characters at a reasonable price. We've created a sleek, intuitive platform that celebrates stories from Star Wars, Marvel, and beyond, delivering a premium viewing experience without the premium cost.
